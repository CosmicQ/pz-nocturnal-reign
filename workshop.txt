title=Nocturnal Reign
description=Zombies fear the sun and rule the night.
description=
description=By day, any zombie caught in direct outdoor sunlight slows to a crippled shamble and retreats indoors - clear a street at noon and it stays clear, but every building becomes a den of lurking dead. Optionally, the sun slowly burns exposed zombies to death.
description=
description=By night, the horde mutates: every zombie becomes a fast, sharp-eyed, keen-eared sprinter. Daytime is for looting. Nighttime is for barricading the doors.
description=
description=[h1]Territorial Lords[/h1]
description=Every major town is the domain of its own named Zombie Lord - "The Lord of Rosewood", "The Lord of Louisville" - an alpha predator immune to daylight, marked by a blood-red glow, dressed in bone armour and a top hat. It rises when a survivor first enters its territory and stalks them within its borders: silently gathering every zombie it passes into a coordinated pack, broadcasting your position to the whole horde the moment it finds you, calling fog to shield its pack from the sun - and, once a day, letting out a blood-curdling shriek that calls a horde clawing up out of the earth around it. The horde scales with the throne: Louisville's Lord raises four times what Rosewood's can. 12 territories across the Build 42 map, tiered 1-4, with Louisville as the endgame domain.
description=
description=Slay a town's Lord to LIBERATE it: its zombies stop mutating at night, permanently or until the Lord respawns (configurable). The campaign state persists across saves and server restarts. Roaming Lords still haunt the wilderness between towns.
description=
description=[h1]The Lord's Chosen[/h1]
description=Towns of tier 2 and up field a court of mini-boss lieutenants, each embodying one of the Lord's powers and stripping it from that town's Lord when slain: the Shepherd (escort pack), the Herald (position broadcast), the Gravedigger (Raise the Dead), and the Brute (the Lord's tier health bonus). The chosen glow ember-amber, each drops a bundle of boss loot - and once per life, a wounded chosen will collapse and feign death among the corpses, lunging when you come close enough to loot it.
description=
description=Boss corpses pay tier-scaled loot: katana, firearms with ammo, gold, an army pack stocked with medical supplies.
description=
description=Every timing, damage, chance and radius value is exposed in Sandbox Options (27 options). Works in new and existing saves. Built and tested on Build 42.19, singleplayer and dedicated server - the mod is architected for B42's unstable multiplayer (day/night rules run on every machine, boss state is broadcast to clients every second). Work in progress - the boss progression continues toward the King of Louisville.
description=
